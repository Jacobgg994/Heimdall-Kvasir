# Thai Market — Hard Data Points

Source-of-truth numbers for sizing decisions. Refresh quarterly.

## Population & connectivity (DataReportal Jan 2024)

| Metric | Value | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| Population | 71.85M | Big enough to scale; small enough to dominate |
| Median age | 40.5 yrs | Older than most ASEAN — buying power skewed to 35–55 |
| Urban share | 53.9% | Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket dominate; rural is LINE-heavy |
| Internet users | 63.21M (88.0%) | Saturation reached — fight for share, not access |
| Mobile connections | 97.81M (136% of pop.) | Multi-SIM; always-online buyer base |
| Mobile internet speed (median) | 40.69 Mbps | Good enough for video shopping |
| Fixed internet speed (median) | 216.26 Mbps | Good enough for live commerce |

## Economy (Wikipedia 2026 estimates)

| Metric | Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| GDP (nominal) | $580B | 31st in the world, 2nd in ASEAN |
| GDP per capita (nominal) | $8,110 | Mid-tier |
| GDP per capita (PPP) | $27,440 | Real spending power is much higher |
| 2025 growth (forecast) | 1.8% | Slow at macro — micro plays are where alpha lives |
| Tourism share of GDP | ~12% (2024 recovery) | Tourist-facing margins are highest |
| Average monthly wage | ฿15,352 (~$437) | Most consumers price-sensitive → high-volume / low-AOV wins |
| Corporate tax rate | 20% | Standard; BOI-eligible activities get 8-year holidays |
| Foreign reserves (Dec 2025) | $306.51B | Macro stability |
| FDI stock (2017) | $205.5B | Inbound capital well-established |

## E-commerce platforms (working knowledge, refresh quarterly)

| Platform | Where it wins |
|---|---|
| **Shopee** | Highest penetration; impulse + low-AOV; commission 1–10% |
| **Lazada** | Electronics, appliances; higher AOV; commission 1–8% |
| **TikTok Shop** | Discovery → buy in-app; commission 5–20%; pays weekly |
| **Facebook Marketplace** | Used goods, local pickup; no native affiliate |
| **LINE Shopping** | Smaller; integrates with LINE OA |
| **JD Central** | Closed in 2023 |

## Payment rails

| Method | Status |
|---|---|
| PromptPay (QR) | Default for ฿200+ payments; instant, free |
| Card (Visa/MC) | Limited adoption outside Bangkok middle class |
| TrueMoney Wallet | Top mobile wallet; integrates with all majors |
| Rabbit LINE Pay | Strong in transport; declining in retail |
| Cash on Delivery | Still significant in rural / first-time buyers |
